[edit] English

The New Year is one of many examples of yearly holidays.

[edit] Etymology

year +‎ -ly

[edit] Pronunciation

  • Audio (US)
    		(file)

[edit] Adjective

yearly (not comparable)

  1. Happening once every year.

[edit] Synonyms

  • annual
  • per annum
  • perennial

[edit] Adverb

yearly (not comparable)

  1. Once a year.
  2. Every year.

[edit] Synonyms

  • annually
  • perennially

[edit] Noun

yearly (plural yearlies)

  1. Something that is published once a year.
