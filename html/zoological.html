[edit] English

[edit] Etymology

From zoology + -ical

[edit] Pronunciation

  • (UK) IPA: /zəʊəˈlɒdʒɪkəl/
  • (US) IPA: /zoʊəˈlɑdʒɪkəl/
  • (US) (non standard) IPA: /zuəˈlɑdʒɪkəl/

[edit] Adjective

zoological (comparative more zoological, superlative most zoological)

  1. Of, or relating to, animals.
  2. Of, or relating to, zoology.

[edit] Derived terms

  • zoological garden
  • zoological name
  • zoological nomenclature
