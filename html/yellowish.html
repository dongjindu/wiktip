[edit] English

[edit] Etymology

yellow +‎ -ish

[edit] Pronunciation

  • (RP) IPA: /ˈjeləʊɪʃ/
  • (US) IPA: /ˈjelɔʊɪʃ/
  • Audio (US)
    		(file)

[edit] Adjective

yellowish (not comparable)

  1. Somewhat yellow (in colour).

[edit] Synonyms

  • yellowy
