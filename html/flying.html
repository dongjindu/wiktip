[edit] English

[edit] Etymology

fly +‎ -ing

[edit] Pronunciation

  • Audio (US)
    		(file)

[edit] Adjective

flying (not comparable)

  1. That which can fly.
    flying fox
  2. Brief or hurried.
    flying visit
  3. (nautical, of a sail) Not secured by yards.

[edit] Verb

MiG-17F flying

flying

  1. Present participle of fly.

[edit] Derived terms

  • flyingly
