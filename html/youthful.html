[edit] English

[edit] Adjective

youthful (comparative more youthful, superlative most youthful)

  1. Young or seeming young.
  2. Characteristic of young people.

[edit] Synonyms

  • lively
  • sprightly
  • spry
  • young

[edit] Derived terms

  • youthful river
