[edit] English

[edit] Etymology

zealous +‎ -ly

[edit] Adverb

zealously (comparative more zealously, superlative most zealously)

  1. Like a zealot; with zealotry.

[edit] Synonyms

  • (with zealotry): eagerly, enthusiastically
