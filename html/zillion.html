[edit] English

[edit] Etymology

See +‎ -illion

[edit] Pronunciation

  • Rhymes: -ɪljən
  • Audio (UK)
    		(file)

[edit] Noun

zillion (plural zillions)

  1. (slang, hyperbolic) An unspecified large number (of).
    When I want to switch Groupwise domains I am forced to click the cancel button on a login dialog box a zillion times (I’m not exaggerating... much)
    • 2008, "Brain tests show child wealth gap", BBC News, 6 December 2008:
      "We are certainly not blaming lower socioeconomic families for not talking to their kids - there are probably a zillion reasons why that happens."
