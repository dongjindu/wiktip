[edit] English

[edit] Pronunciation

  • enPR: yo͞od, IPA: /juːd/, X-SAMPA: /ju:d/
  • Audio (US)
    		(file)
  • Rhymes: -uːd

[edit] Contraction

you'd

  1. (informal) You had.
  2. (informal) You should.
  3. (informal) You would.
