[edit] Alternative forms

  • (rare) zoölogist

[edit] Etymology

zoology +‎ -ist

[edit] Pronunciation

  • (UK) IPA: /zuːˈɒlədʒɪst/
  • (US) IPA: /zoʊˈɑlədʒɪst/
  • (US) (non standard) IPA: /zuˈɑlədʒɪst/

[edit] Noun

zoologist (plural zoologists)

  1. Person who studies zoology.
