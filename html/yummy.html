[edit] English

[edit] Pronunciation

  • (UK) IPA: /ˈjʌm.i/, X-SAMPA: /"jVm.i/
  • (US) IPA: /ˈjəmij/, X-SAMPA: /"j@mij/
  • Audio (US)
    		(file)
  • Rhymes: -ʌmi

[edit] Adjective

yummy (comparative yummier, superlative yummiest)

  1. (colloquial, lighthearted, often childish) delicious.
