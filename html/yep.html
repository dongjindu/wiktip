[edit] English

[edit] Etymology

Representing yes pronounced with the mouth snapped closed at the end.

[edit] Pronunciation

  • IPA: /jɛp/, /jɛp̚/
  • Rhymes: -ɛp

[edit] Adverb

yep (not comparable)

  1. Yes.

[edit] Synonyms

  • See also Wikisaurus:yes

[edit] Antonyms

  • nope

[edit] Noun

yep (plural yeps)

  1. (informal) Yes.
    I'll take that as a yep, then.
