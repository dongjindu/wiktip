[edit] English

[edit] Pronunciation

  • Rhymes: -ɜː(r)nɪŋ

[edit] Noun

yearning (plural yearnings)

  1. A wistful or melancholy longing.
    She had a yearning to see her long-lost sister again.
  2. (archaic) rennet
