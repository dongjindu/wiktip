[edit] English

[edit] Alternative forms

  • yukky

[edit] Adjective

yucky (comparative yuckier, superlative yuckiest)

  1. (colloquial, often childish) Of something highly offensive; causing aversion or disgust.

[edit] Synonyms

  • disgusting
  • distasteful
  • foul
  • loathsome
  • nasty
  • offensive
  • repugnant
  • revolting
  • unpleasant
  • wicked

[edit] Antonyms

  • yummy

[edit] Derived terms

  • yuckiness

[edit] Related terms

  • yuck, yuk

[edit] Translations

causing aversion or disgust
  • Finnish: kuvottava (fi)
  • Italian: schifoso (it)
  • Scottish Gaelic: caca (gd)

[edit] Quotations

"Don't rule out desalination because it is expensive, or recycling because it sounds yucky, or building a dam," Mr Turnbull told Australian media." — BBC Online
