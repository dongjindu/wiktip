[edit] Etymology

From year +‎ book. Compare West Frisian jierboek (“yearbook”), Dutch jaarboek (“yearbook”), German Jahrbuch (“yearbook”), Swedish årsbok (“yearbook”), Faroese árbók (“annal, record”).

[edit] Noun

yearbook (plural yearbooks)

  1. a reference book, published annually
  2. (US) A publication compiled by the graduating class of a high school or college, recording the year's events and containing photographs of students and faculty
