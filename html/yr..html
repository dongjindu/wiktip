[edit] English

[edit] Abbreviation

yr

  1. year
  2. your

[edit] Norwegian

[edit] Pronunciation

  • IPA: /yːr/

[edit] Adjective

yr

  1. cheerful, jolly, merry

[edit] Noun

yr n.

  1. drizzle

[edit] Old English

[edit] Etymology

Probably borrowed from Old Norse ýr ‘yew, a bow, the runic letter ᛦ’.

[edit] Pronunciation

  • IPA: /yːr/

[edit] Noun

ȳr

  1. the runic letter ᚣ (/y/).
