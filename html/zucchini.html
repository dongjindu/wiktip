[edit] English

some zucchini

[edit] Etymology

From Italian zucchini, plural of zucchino.

[edit] Pronunciation

  • Audio (US)
    		(file)

[edit] Noun

zucchini (plural zucchinis or zucchini) (Australia, Canada, New Zealand, US)

  1. (uncountable) A particular variety of Cucurbita pepo, a squash/marrow.
  2. (countable) A fruit of this variety.
  3. (uncountable) This fruit, considered as a food; or, a dish made from it.

[edit] Synonyms

  • courgette (British)

[edit] Related terms

  • zucchetto
